About Us

Welcome to our online watch store! We are first-year Bachelor's students at Islington College, Kathmandu, pursuing BSc. (Hons) in Computing. This website is part of our Information Systems module project, where we collaborate as a team of five. Each member is responsible for designing one of the five pages, ensuring a blend of creativity and functionality using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and wireframes. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform that showcases our skills and dedication. Thank you for visiting our project. We hope you enjoy exploring our work as much as we enjoyed creating it!
